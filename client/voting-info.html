Are you eligible to vote?

  • Must be a United States citizen.
  • Must be at least 18 years old on or before Election Day.
  • Must be a resident of Dublin, CA.
  • Must be registered to vote at least 15 days before the election.
  • Cannot be currently incarcerated for a felony conviction.
  • Must not be found mentally incompetent to vote by a court.

How to vote:

  • Option 1: Drop off your paper at 24-hour Ballot Drop Boxes: Dublin Civic Center (100 Civic Plaza) and The Wave (4201 Central Parkway)
  • Option 2: Voting Centers
    • 11-Day Voting Centers: The Wave (4201 Central Parkway)
      • Open: October 26 - November 4, 2024, 9AM to 5PM
      • Open: November 5, 2024, 7AM to 8PM
    • 4-Day Voting Centers: Civic Center (100 Civic Plaza), John Knox Presbyterian Church (7421 Amarillo Road), Santa Rita Public Works (4825 Gleason Drive)
      • Open: November 2 - November 4, 2024, 9AM to 5PM
      • Open: November 5, 2024, 7AM to 8PM
