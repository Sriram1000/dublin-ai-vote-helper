Dublin Mayor Candidates

Tom Evans

Lived in Dublin for 40 years, running to oppose poorly planned projects and advocate for better planning and growth control.

Sherry Hu

Vice Mayor, serving on city council for four years. Focuses on balancing the budget, supporting local businesses, and enhancing public safety.

Jean Josey

Long-time resident and councilmember focused on sustainable growth, affordable housing, and improving public safety services.

Shawn Costello

Advocate for disabled rights and affordable housing, with 30 years of experience in public service despite his own physical challenges.
